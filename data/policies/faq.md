# Frequently Asked Questions

This page pulls together the questions our support team hears most. Many answers point you to a fuller policy, because the detail lives there and we would rather not keep two copies of a rule that could drift apart.

**How long do I have to get my money back?**
Fourteen days from delivery for a refund. If you want to send an item back you actually have thirty days to start a return, and the refund follows the return. Damaged items are the fourteen-day refund case and need a photo.

**Where is my order?**
Ask us in chat and we will read your order's current status and its estimated delivery date. An order moves from created to approved to shipped to delivered, and once it is delivered your refund and return windows begin.

**My order is late — can I get a refund for that?**
A delivery counts as late once it passes the estimated delivery date. A short delay is usually the carrier catching up. If it is well past the estimate, contact us and we will open a ticket to investigate; a genuinely lost order is resolved with a replacement or refund.

**Can I cancel my order?**
Only before it ships. Once the status is shipped it is with the carrier and cannot be cancelled — after that, use a return or refund when it arrives.

**Do I need to send a photo?**
Only when the item is damaged or defective. Those refund and replacement requests need a photo before we approve them; other requests do not.

**What is the difference between a refund, a return, and a replacement?**
A refund is money back. A return is the goods coming back to us, which usually leads to a refund. A replacement is the same item sent again, and it is only for defective or wrong items. Refunds run on fourteen days, returns and replacements on thirty.

**Something broke months after I bought it — is it too late?**
Not necessarily. The warranty covers manufacturing defects for twelve months from delivery. It does not cover accidental damage, misuse, or normal wear.

**Who pays return shipping?**
We do, if the item is defective or we sent the wrong one. You do, if you changed your mind.

**Why is my refund slower than my friend's?**
Refund speed depends on how you paid — cards come back within five business days, bank slip payments take up to ten because they are refunded by bank transfer.

**I paid with a voucher and a card — why two refunds?**
Orders paid with more than one method are refunded to each method in proportion, so more than one entry is normal.
