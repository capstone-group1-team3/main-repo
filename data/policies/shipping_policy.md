# Shipping & Delivery Policy

This page explains how we ship, how to track an order, and what happens when a delivery runs late. Delivery timing also matters for your other rights: your refund and return windows are both counted from the delivery date, so the date recorded here is the same date those clocks start from.

## Estimated delivery

Every order shows an estimated delivery date at checkout, and that same estimate is stored on your order so you and our support team see the same number. The estimate is a good-faith projection, not a guarantee, and busy periods or carrier delays can push it. When you ask "where is my order?", our support system reads your order's current status and its estimated delivery date straight from your record and tells you where things stand.

## Tracking your order

An order moves through several states — created, approved, shipped, and delivered — and you can see the current one at any time from your account or by asking in chat. Once an order is marked shipped it is with the carrier and on its way; once it is delivered, the delivery date is recorded and your refund and return windows begin.

## When a delivery is late

A delivery is considered late when the actual delivery date passes the estimated delivery date on your order. A short delay is usually just the carrier catching up, and we ask for a little patience before treating it as a problem. If an order is significantly past its estimate, contact support and we will open a ticket to investigate; genuinely lost shipments are resolved with a replacement or a refund depending on stock and your preference.

## Cancelling before it ships

You can cancel an order only **before it has shipped**. Once an order's status is shipped, it is with the carrier and can no longer be cancelled — at that point your route is to wait for delivery and then use a return or a refund if the item is not what you wanted. This is why acting quickly matters if you change your mind right after ordering.

## Shipping costs and refunds of shipping

Whether shipping charges come back to you when something goes wrong is covered in the Refund and Return policies rather than here, but the short version is that we refund shipping when the fault is ours and not when you simply change your mind.

## Quick answers

*How do I know when my order arrives?* Check the estimated delivery date on your order, or ask support and we will read your order's status and estimate for you.

*My order is late — what now?* If it is only a little past the estimate, give the carrier a short while. If it is well past, contact us and we will open a ticket.

*Can I cancel?* Only before the order ships. After it ships, use a return or refund once it arrives.
